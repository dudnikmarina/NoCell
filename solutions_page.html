How it works

NoCellTM controls and limits cell phone use for the driver—but not for passengers. ZoneTagTM, an unobtrusive BluetoothTM vehicle ID puck placed on the driver-side windshield, connects to a linked cell phone and automatically disables unauthorized Apps. App permissions and system alerts are fully customizable per company policy, enabling enforcement and keeping drivers safe, sound and devoid of distraction.

Product Overview

Enforce Your Policy.
Improve Their Safety.

Distracted driving is a growing concern—but NoCellTM can help protect your employees, your assets, and your company.

The danger associated with cell phone use while driving is widely documented. NoCell, an enterprise-grade solution, is designed with a singular purpose—to help keep your driver safe and free from mobile device distractions.

Whether you have three—or three thousand—vehicles in your fleet, our proprietary platform effectively minimizes cell phone usage and enhances compliance with your company policy. The system is comprised of three components—a smartphone app, an online administrator portal for monitoring and storing driver data, and a ZoneTagTM.

The ZoneTagTM detects vehicle motion and interfaces with the driver’s phone (but not passengers’ mobile devices) during work hours, to automatically reduce distractions.

Available features include:
  • Removing apps from view
  • Silencing phone alerts
  • Routing calls to voicemail (option)
  • Delaying text messages
  • Tracking driver location (option)
  • Collecting driver data

Administrators receive alerts and near real-time driver data through our Administrator Portal. The Portal is easy to configure and use, and provides both at-a-glance metrics and comprehensive reports for a deeper dive.

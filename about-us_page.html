ABOUT US

OUR STORY

NoCell TM develops technologies that mitigate the leading cause of distracted driving – the use of cell phones. The enterprise-grade NoCell TM platform provides driver-specific, customer-defined cell phone usage policy enforcement.

NoCell partners with leading providers of telematics, mobility services, InsurTech, fleet and field management systems, plus mobile carriers, to enrich the value of their solutions, increase road safety, reduce commercial liability and prevent loss.

Headquartered in Aliso Viejo, CA, NoCell TM was founded in 2017 by a group of technology experts looking to make a real difference in driving safety.

MISSION

Our mission is to be a global leader in decreasing vehicle crashes, injuries and fatalities due to electronic device distraction. We designed our technology to limit cell phone distraction while driving, and inform fleet supervisors of policy non-compliance with corporate safety rules and policies.

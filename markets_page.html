Mobile Network Operators

Mobile carriers & virtual network operators (MVNO) worldwide that sell private-label or acquired fleet management and ELD services, field service apps and usage-based insurance (UBI) solutions are under pressure to repeatedly gain market share. Furthermore, they are increasingly compelled to maintain or increase Average Selling Price (ASP) & Average Revenue Per User (ARPU), all while reducing churn.

NoCell’s enterprise-grade platform is designed from the ground up to rapidly integrate with all these existing fleet telematics solutions. Carriers/MVNO’s integrate and private-label NoCell under their own brands; furthermore, they can sell it as an add-on service for current customers, and up-sell or bundle to gain new subscribers at higher ASP/ARPU.
